## Mission Statement

At Queer Consent Corner, we strive to create a safe and welcoming community for all queer individuals to learn, discuss, and practice trauma-informed consent negotiations for sex and kink play. We prioritize education, accountability, and demonstration of explicit consent practices that foster a culture of respect and care.
